☕ Dalgona Coffee Recipe ☕

The yummy South Korean coffee delight that has been a huge hit all over the internet
which surprisingly only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you might have in our cupboard
- now you feel like you have won a jackpot right

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

The process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water:
Take 2 tablespoon of instant coffe, 2 tablespoon of sugar, 2 tablespoon of hot water and put all of it in a
mixing bowl

Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
Wisk all of your ingredients and keep wisking till light and fluffy
Tip: After a few seconds to one
minuite of wisking if your coffee starts bubbling up you can continue if it is not bubbling up then,
STOP !!

Step 3: Serve with Milk
Using a spoon granish your cold milk with the whipped up Coffee
